About DBCA Global

Born from hustle. Built with heart. Driven by one belief:
That precision, integrity, and accountability are the foundation of sustainable growth.

DBCA Global is a U.S.-based supply chain and consulting company specializing in procurement, distribution, and representation of international raw materials for manufacturing and specialty chemical industries.

Founded by Donis Bosworth, a 20-year veteran of global trade and former Vice President of North American Minerals Corporation, DBCA Global was established to bring clarity and reliability to complex international supply chains.

What We Do

We operate at the intersection of manufacturing, logistics, and strategy — connecting global suppliers with trusted North American partners. Our work spans:

  • Procurement and Representation: Building and managing exclusive partnerships between international producers and U.S. manufacturers.
  • Supply Chain Optimization: Handling import logistics, warehousing, and national distribution with full compliance and cost control.
  • Strategic Consulting: Guiding global suppliers on U.S. market entry, financing, and partnership models.
  • Operational Support: Providing infrastructure for consignment programs, bonded storage, and just-in-time delivery solutions.

DBCA Global manages material flow and compliance from origin to end-user, reducing risk while increasing transparency and performance across the chain.
