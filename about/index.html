About DBCA Global

Austin, TX – October 2025 — DBCA Global, LLC officially launches as a U.S.-based procurement and logistics consultancy redefining how international suppliers connect with American manufacturers.

Founded by Donis Bosworth, a 20-year veteran of North American Minerals Corporation (NAMCO), DBCA Global brings precision, transparency, and trust to an industry in urgent need of resilience.

"Supply chains are strained, and U.S. manufacturers need more than vendors — they need partners who deliver with integrity and speed. DBCA exists to remove friction from trade. We don't just optimize pipelines — we humanize them."

— Donis Bosworth, Founder & Managing Director

Our Mission

DBCA was born from hustle, built with heart, and driven by one belief: we do what others say can't be done.

This isn't just another consultancy — it's a movement toward a smarter, more transparent supply chain for America. We build trusted partnerships, deliver seamless logistics, and produce real results.

Backed by Bosworth's two decades of procurement leadership, DBCA offers a unique low-risk, high-margin model that builds trust at every step of the supply chain.

Our Founder

Donis Bosworth
